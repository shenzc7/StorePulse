# StorePulse Demo Script - 3-Minute Walkthrough

## OVERVIEW
**Goal:** Demonstrate complete StorePulse workflow in 3 minutes
**Audience:** Retail executives and operations managers
**Setup:** Pre-loaded with sample data, models pre-trained
**Timing:** Exactly 180 seconds (3 minutes)

---

## TIMING BREAKDOWN

### 0:00 - 0:30 (30 sec) | LAUNCH & INTRODUCTION
**"Let's see StorePulse in action. I'll walk you through the complete forecasting process in just 3 minutes."**

[LAUNCH APPLICATION]
- Click StorePulse icon on desktop
- Show clean, professional interface loading
- Highlight status indicators: "API • Local • SQLite"

**Key Message:** "StorePulse works completely offline - your data never leaves your computer."

---

### 0:30 - 0:45 (15 sec) | HOME DASHBOARD
**"Here's what StorePulse shows when you first open it."**

[NAVIGATE HOME PAGE]
- Show AccuracyMeter with current model performance
- Display StatusDock with system health
- Highlight ActionCard for key actions

**Key Message:** "Real-time dashboard shows model accuracy and system status."

---

### 0:45 - 1:15 (30 sec) | DATA UPLOAD
**"First, we upload your historical visit data. StorePulse accepts standard CSV files."**

[NAVIGATE TO DATA PAGE]
- Click "Data" in navigation
- Show upload interface with drag-and-drop area
- Demonstrate file selection (pre-prepared sample file)

[SHOW DATA VALIDATION]
- Display automatic column detection
- Show data preview with validation results
- Highlight "365 records loaded successfully"

**Key Message:** "Flexible data import handles your existing store formats automatically."

---

### 1:15 - 1:45 (30 sec) | MODEL TRAINING
**"Now we train the forecasting models on your data. This usually takes 5-15 minutes, but watch how it works."**

[NAVIGATE TO TRAIN PAGE]
- Click "Train" in navigation
- Show training configuration options
- Select "Quick Demo" mode for speed

[START TRAINING - SHOW PROGRESS]
- Click "Start Training" button
- Demonstrate live progress updates
- Show SSE progress streaming: "Loading data... Training model... Validating..."

**Key Message:** "Advanced ML analyzes your patterns to build accurate forecasting models."

---

### 1:45 - 2:15 (30 sec) | VIEW FORECASTS
**"Here's the result - 14-day visit forecasts with business recommendations."**

[NAVIGATE TO FORECAST PAGE]
- Click "Forecast" in navigation (or auto-navigate after training)
- Show forecast chart with P10/P50/P90 bands
- Highlight prediction intervals and confidence levels

[SHOW BUSINESS RECOMMENDATIONS]
- Display staffing suggestions: "Schedule 4 staff for peak days"
- Show inventory alerts: "Monitor dairy products - high demand expected"
- Demonstrate uncertainty bands for decision-making

**Key Message:** "P10/P50/P90 bands help you plan for best case, most likely, and worst case scenarios."

---

### 2:15 - 2:45 (30 sec) | WHAT-IF SCENARIOS
**"StorePulse lets you test business scenarios before implementation."**

[NAVIGATE TO LAB PAGE]
- Click "Lab" in navigation
- Show scenario creation interface
- Create "Holiday Promotion" scenario

[COMPARE SCENARIOS]
- Show baseline vs. scenario forecasts side-by-side
- Display impact analysis: "+25% visits expected"
- Highlight staffing and inventory impacts

**Key Message:** "Test promotions, weather changes, or competitive scenarios before deciding."

---

### 2:45 - 3:00 (15 sec) | EXPORT REPORTS
**"Finally, generate professional reports for your team."**

[NAVIGATE TO REPORTS PAGE]
- Click "Reports" in navigation
- Show export options and PDF generation
- Demonstrate report download

[SHOW REPORT PREVIEW]
- Display executive-ready PDF format
- Highlight charts, metrics, and recommendations

**Key Message:** "Export professional PDFs with all forecasts and business recommendations."
